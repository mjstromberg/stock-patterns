Healthy Cup Definition

  • Cup Length:

    Cups should last between 7 and 65 weeks long, but are typically between 12 and 48 weeks.

  • Cup Depth:

    Measured realtive to the previous high, cups should only see a 12% to 33% decrease to be considered healthy.

  • Prior Uptrend:

    Prior uptrends need to have an increase of at least 30% from the previous low, and they should also see a substantial increase in trading volume along the way.
